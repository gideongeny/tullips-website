About Tullips Creative Green Spaces

Bringing nature's beauty to your doorstep

At Tullips Creative Green Spaces, we believe every space deserves beauty and life. Based in Kericho, Kenya, we specialize in fresh flowers, plants, natural stonework, and creative landscaping touches. Our mission is to combine nature and creativity, offering handcrafted designs that are timeless, elegant, and affordable.
